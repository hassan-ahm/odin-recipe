Noodles

Description

have you ever found yourself wide awake in the middle of the night really hungry, well we have all been there, but dont worry this hardy noodle recipe is quick to make and will satisfy your midnight cravings.

Ingredients

  • noodle packet
  • water

Steps

  1. pour 200ml of water in a pot and put it on a stove
  2. once the water is boiling chuck in the instant noodle packet
  3. open the seasoning sachet and add that to the pot and stir
  4. wait for the noodles to cook ~ 2 mins, be careful not to overcook them
  5. pour the noodles in a bowl and enjoy
