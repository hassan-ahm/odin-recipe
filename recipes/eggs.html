Eggs

Description

this scrambled egg recipe is perfect for a quick and hardy breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • butter

Steps

  1. put 1 tb of butter in a frying pan
  2. put the pan on a stove and wait for the butter to melt
  3. crack 3 eggs in a bowl and whick them
  4. pour the eggs into the pan and stir often
  5. stir until the eggs are cooked
